DIAL CHARLIEPLEX NET ASSIGNMENT
================================
13 nets: CP0..CP12  (each = one MCU pin through one ~47ohm series resistor)
Each LED Dn: anode -> net_anode, cathode -> net_cathode  (see dial_placement.csv)
Antiparallel partner of (CPa anode, CPb cathode) is the LED with (CPb anode, CPa cathode).

Assign these in the SCHEMATIC (net labels on each LED), then import netlist to the PCB.
The placement script positions parts; nets come from the schematic.
